

# Tic-Tac-Toe
This is a Tic-tac-toe game for two players, X and O, who take turns marking the spaces in a 3Ã—3 grid. The player who succeeds in placing three of their marks in a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal row wins the game. In this game "O" is AI player and "X" is human player.
